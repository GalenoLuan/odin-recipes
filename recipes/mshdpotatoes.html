Mashed Potatoes

These garlic mashed potatoes are rich and very tasty! For a creamier texture, use heavy cream in place of the milk. These are also terrific topped with sesame seeds or green onions.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil and drain the potatoes and garlic
  2. Mash or beat them with the remaining ingredients.
  3. Adding the butter first to the hot potatoes allows you to use less milk/cream and keeps the potatoes creamier for longer.
  4. Stir in some sour cream and chives to add extra creaminess and flavor!
